ACS-85-0002 Two Frequency VCO  Github

Two controllable frequencies and a pin that is a two tone combination. Values from 0 to 5 volts on the input pins produces audio squarewaves on the output pins. The final pin alternates between the two frequencies at a fixed interval.

ACS-85-0003 allows you to play with the alternating frequencies more. .
