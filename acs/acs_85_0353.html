ACS-85-0353 Sample and modulate with LFSR  Github

Similar to if you connected a VCO to a psycho lfo

Takes a medium to High frequency on pin 7 and outputs a squarewave with variable frequency and width. Kinda like the frequency was modulated by a square wave source.

The Width determines the size of the mask to add to the sampled frequency. Speed is the duration of each step.
