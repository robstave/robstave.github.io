ACS-85-0550 Melody Maker Upper 1  Github

One control for speed. Notes are played on three pins with each pin updating in a round robin fashion.

AND/OR/XOR them or or just sum them using resistors.

Similar to ACS-85-0550. This is not like the 4017/4051 Melody Generator

This is a sample of all 3 pins summed up and playing at the same time. you could easily send each pin to its own destination or whatever.
