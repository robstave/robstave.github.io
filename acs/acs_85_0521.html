ACS-85-0521 16 bit LFSR Internal Frequency  Github

Externally clocked 16 bit Linear Feedback Shift Register. The values at certain taps are xor'ed together and feed back into the shift register. The number of unique states should be 2^n -1. In this case 2^16 -1 = 65535.

Basically, similar to ACS-85-0520, but the frequency is set with a analog value on A1. The frequency is in the audio range
