ACS-85-0008 Squarewave with Vibrato  Github

Two inputs. One for Freq and the other for Vibrato width. Vibrato is when you modulate the frequency. All the sketches from 85-0006 to 85-0008 are variations of that. The best sounding one is the final Triangle modulation on PB2.

The outputs are

  • PB0 - Frequency ramps down
  • PB1 - Frequency Ramps Up
  • PB2 - Frequency goes up and down as a Triangle wave
